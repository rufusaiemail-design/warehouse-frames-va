Our Story

Family owned.
Houston proud.

Founded by Don and Teri Waheed, Warehouse Frames 'n Gallery has grown into Houston's largest frame destination. Their sons Mark and Gregg have been an integral part of the business for over 25 years — and their hands-on approach shows in every visit.

Today the business occupies over 34,000 sq ft across two buildings, with the main showroom at 3815 Fondren Road. We import container loads from five countries and maintain over 20,000 frames — all at factory direct prices.
